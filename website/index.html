Our Plan

What's the idea ?

Let’s ask ourselves: when we are out with our friends late at night, are we safe? If we are then why are our loved ones sitting at home worrying until we’re home? Let’s face it: we have made peace with the level of crime in our society and don’t blink an eye when we hear of a new incident. We live with eve-teasing and hence even embolden attackers. Safe Route is the first stage of empowering ourselves by avoiding any such scenarios where we’d be at risk. Using a layer of technology on top of proliferating smart devices, and the youth’s desire to be safe and free, this project aims at developing crowdsourced safe routes for our mundane commutes.

Who is our user?

Our primary audience is women. The Indian set up is almost satirical: on the one hand we worship a fleet of Goddesses for education, money, health, wealth and anything under the sun. On the other hand, the average girl on the street is treated as fair bait. Why? Empowering this audience is our primary motive. We see our app also being used by tight-knit families who worry until their kids are home ; the father who eagerly wants to get home safely to his family; the grandpa who is looking for a leisurely but safe stroll through his neighbourhood. Our design is simple and adaptable by almost anyone

Why now?

First, rampant media coverage has brought these shameful incidents to a global arena: India has awakened with the realization that our system is broken; our values are archaic. The youth has taken to streets grieving at every incident reported. There is a sense of moral ownership to fix the core issue at heart. Secondly, technology is at its prime : there is an android phone in the common man’s hand . Using existing open platforms like Google Maps and Android developer kit we plan to deliver an intuitive interface that can be woven into the fabric of our day to day lives.
