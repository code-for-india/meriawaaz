Neena Budhiraja

Neena is a product manager on Google's brand display team. She has led the conception and development of engagement ads, and is focused on making rich media advertising easy and measurable. Since joining Google in 2009, Neena has worked as chief of staff for the display ads team at Google, and has also forecasted revenues as a financial analyst.

Neena earned her bachelor's of science degree in computer science from Panjab University, India, and an MBA from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh.
